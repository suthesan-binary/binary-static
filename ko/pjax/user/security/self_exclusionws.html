Self-Exclusion

Online trading can be addictive. Self-exclusion is a facility to limit your online trading activity, should you need it. You can set limits in your account to help prevent unwanted losses. You may also instruct us to exclude you from trading on Binary.com for a specific period of time. This self-exclusion date cannot be amended once it is set.
